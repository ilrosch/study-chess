Group 1 project: Learning to play chess

Chess is one of the most fascinating and intellectual games ever created. Despite its popularity, many people do not know how to play it. Our group decided to create a project aimed at teaching the game of chess.

We have built on the successful methodologies used in the learning sites recommended by our teachers, such as Grid Garden and Flex Frog. Using their approaches, we aim to make the process of learning chess interesting and accessible to all.
